Just pause

There’s always been that person who kinda figured things out.
Had the plan. The vision. And most times? It worked.
Not perfectly, but enough to keep going. To stay sure.

Then comes that moment—
when they no longer know what direction they’re facing.
The plan fades. The decisions feel heavier.
And suddenly, they’re not figuring things out anymore.

What do you do then?
When the clarity is gone, and all that’s left is the question.

Maybe… you pause.
Not because you’ve failed, but because you’ve outgrown the old answers.
Maybe this not-knowing isn’t the end—
just the space before something new begins.
